---
title: "The cost of options"
date: 2026-03-30
type: note
tags: [design, decisions]
description: "Every preference is a tax on every user."
draft: false
---

Every preference you add is a decision the user now has to make. Most of the time, they don't want to. They want the thing to work.

The right default beats the right option. If you find yourself adding a setting because two people on your team disagreed, you have not resolved the disagreement — you have shipped it to the user.
