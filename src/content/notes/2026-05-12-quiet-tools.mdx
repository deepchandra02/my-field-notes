The best tools I use are the ones I forget I'm using. A good text editor disappears. A good keyboard disappears. A good terminal disappears.

This is the opposite of how tools tend to be marketed — every feature surfaced, every preference exposed. But the *experience* of a good tool is that it stays out of the way until you reach for it.

Worth asking, of anything you build: what would it take for the user to stop noticing this exists?
